Travel ancillary refers to the additional products and services that are offered to travelers beyond their basic travel requirements such as flights, accommodations, and transportation. These ancillary products can include travel insurance, car rentals, airport transfers, tours and activities, and more. They are designed to enhance the overall travel experience by providing convenience, comfort, and added value for travelers. Many airlines and online travel agencies offer these ancillary products as a way to generate additional revenue and improve customer satisfaction. By offering a range of ancillary products, travel companies can tailor their offerings to meet the unique needs and preferences of individual travelers, making their trip more enjoyable and memorable.
